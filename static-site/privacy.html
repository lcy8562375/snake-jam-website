Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

policy 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Shenyang Time Acceleration Technology Co., Ltd. ("Time Acceleration", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our website, mobile games, and related support services.

Our goal is to keep our casual games and public website simple. We do not require users to create an account on our website, and we do not ask users to submit sensitive personal information in order to browse our website.

business 2. Company Information

The company responsible for this Privacy Policy is Shenyang Time Acceleration Technology Co., Ltd. Our website is https://www.timeaccel.com. Our official contact email for privacy requests is contact@timeaccel.com.

database 3. Information We Collect

We may collect limited information in the following situations, depending on how you use our website or games:

  • Support information: if you contact us, we may receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message.
  • Usage and technical data: our website hosting, mobile games, and app platforms may process technical data such as IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, access time, pages visited, gameplay activity, crash reports, or diagnostic information.
  • Platform and SDK information: mobile app stores, device platforms, and third-party SDK providers may process limited information needed for app distribution, security, analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or platform operations, depending on the SDKs used in our games.

We do not currently require website visitors to register an account, provide a phone number, provide a postal address, or sign in with a social media profile.

cookie 4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website may use essential cookies or similar technologies that are necessary for security, basic site functionality, performance, and hosting. We do not currently use cookies for remarketing or behavioral advertising on this website.

You can set your browser to refuse cookies or notify you when a cookie is being sent. Some parts of the website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

psychology 5. How We Use Information

We may use information for the following purposes:

  • to provide, maintain, and improve our website and games;
  • to respond to support requests and user inquiries;
  • to monitor service stability, understand gameplay performance, diagnose bugs, and fix crashes;
  • to protect the security and integrity of our services;
  • to comply with legal, regulatory, and platform obligations.

share 6. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information only when necessary in the following situations:

  • Service providers: with companies that help us operate our website, provide hosting, distribute apps, provide analytics or advertising technology, process diagnostics, or respond to support requests.
  • Legal and safety reasons: when required by law, valid legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of Time Acceleration, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

inventory_2 7. Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including providing support, maintaining security, resolving disputes, and complying with legal obligations.

Support emails may be kept for as long as needed to respond to your request and maintain a record of the issue. Technical logs and diagnostic data are generally retained for a limited period unless a longer period is required for security, troubleshooting, or legal compliance.

public 8. International Transfers

Because our website and games may be accessed internationally, your information may be processed in countries or regions other than where you live. Where required, we take reasonable steps to protect information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable law.
